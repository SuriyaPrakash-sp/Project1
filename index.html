Who We Are

About Our College

Arivagam School of Nursing is a modern institution dedicated to providing exceptional education and healthcare research facilities. With experienced faculty, world-class infrastructure, and a focus on innovation, we prepare students for real-world challenges in nursing and medical laboratory technology.

Our Mission

To train compassionate, competent healthcare professionals equipped with the clinical skill and ethical grounding that patients deserve.

Our Vision

To be a recognised centre of healthcare education in Tamil Nadu, known for outcomes, integrity, and student care.

Our Journey

Milestones so far

2023

Arivagam School of Nursing is founded with a focus on GNM and DMLT education.

2024

Modern labs and library facilities completed; first batch of students admitted.

2025

First graduating cohort of GNM and DMLT students; student hostel opens.

2026

Enrollment grows to 50+ students across two professional programs.

Our Courses
